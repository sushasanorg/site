Who We Are

About Sushasan

Sushasan is an independent, non-partisan civic platform dedicated to improving governance in Nepal. We believe that true democracy requires active citizen engagement, evidence-based dialogue, and absolute transparency in public institutions. Our focus is on systems and accountability, not political parties.

The Three Pillars

Leadership (नेतृत्व)

Promoting ethical, visionary, and inclusive leadership across all tiers of governance. We advocate for leaders who prioritize national interest over personal or partisan gains.

Management (व्यवस्थापन)

Encouraging efficient, merit-based, and responsive public administration. We focus on systemic reforms that improve service delivery to citizens.

Accountability (जवाफदेहिता)

Ensuring public officials answer for decisions affecting citizens. We support mechanisms that prevent corruption and protect institutional integrity.

Questions for Good Governance

We pose critical, evidence-based questions to public institutions to drive transparency. Explore our active inquiries or submit your own.

Finance

How transparent are national infrastructure budgets?

Investigating the accessibility of detailed expenditure reports for major national pride projects.

Procurement

What mechanisms ensure accountability in public procurement?

Analyzing the effectiveness of current e-bidding processes and conflict-of-interest regulations.

Institutions

How are constitutional institutions protected from political interference?

Reviewing the appointment processes and operational independence of key watchdog agencies.

The Sushasan Charter

Our movement is guided by unwavering principles that place the citizen at the center of the state. We commit to:

  • Absolute Transparency in public affairs.
  • Uncompromising Accountability of elected and appointed officials.
  • Upholding the Rule of Law without exception.
  • Meaningful Citizen Participation in policy making.
  • Fostering Ethical Leadership across all sectors.
